# 断肢保存方法详解 📦

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-6 bg-green-50 rounded-xl border-2 border-green-200">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-green-700 mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
<span class="text-2xl">✅</span> 正确保存方法
</h3>

<div class="space-y-3">

<div class="flex gap-3 items-start">
<span class="flex-shrink-0 w-7 h-7 rounded-full bg-green-500 text-white flex items-center justify-center text-sm font-bold">1</span>
<div class="text-sm"><strong>清洁断肢表面</strong> - 用生理盐水或清水轻轻冲洗，去除明显污物</div>
</div>

<div class="flex gap-3 items-start">
<span class="flex-shrink-0 w-7 h-7 rounded-full bg-green-500 text-white flex items-center justify-center text-sm font-bold">2</span>
<div class="text-sm"><strong>包裹断肢</strong> - 用无菌纱布或干净布料包好（稍微湿润）</div>
</div>

<div class="flex gap-3 items-start">
<span class="flex-shrink-0 w-7 h-7 rounded-full bg-green-500 text-white flex items-center justify-center text-sm font-bold">3</span>
<div class="text-sm"><strong>第一层塑料袋</strong> - 放入塑料袋，<span v-mark.underline.green>密封</span>（防止泡水）</div>
</div>

<div class="flex gap-3 items-start">
<span class="flex-shrink-0 w-7 h-7 rounded-full bg-green-500 text-white flex items-center justify-center text-sm font-bold">4</span>
<div class="text-sm"><strong>冰水混合物</strong> - 准备一个容器，装入冰水混合物（1:1）</div>
</div>

<div class="flex gap-3 items-start">
<span class="flex-shrink-0 w-7 h-7 rounded-full bg-green-500 text-white flex items-center justify-center text-sm font-bold">5</span>
<div class="text-sm"><strong>第二层保护</strong> - 将密封袋放入冰水中，外面再套一层袋子</div>
</div>

<div class="flex gap-3 items-start">
<span class="flex-shrink-0 w-7 h-7 rounded-full bg-green-500 text-white flex items-center justify-center text-sm font-bold">6</span>
<div class="text-sm"><strong>标记时间</strong> - 在外包装写上受伤时间</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-blue-100 rounded-lg text-center">
<div class="text-5xl mb-2">📦</div>
<div class="font-bold text-blue-800">三层保护法</div>
<div class="text-sm mt-1 text-blue-700">纱布 → 塑料袋 → 冰水</div>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-6 bg-red-50 rounded-xl border-2 border-red-200">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-red-700 mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
<span class="text-2xl">❌</span> 错误做法（致命！）
</h3>

<div class="space-y-3">

<div class="p-3 bg-white rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-400 text-sm">
<strong class="text-red-600">❌ 直接泡在水里</strong> - 会导致组织<span v-mark.highlight.red>水肿、坏死</span>！<br/>
<span class="text-xs text-gray-600">（必须用塑料袋隔水！）</span>

<span class="text-xs text-gray-600">（一定要用袋子隔开！）</span>

<span class="text-xs text-gray-600">（必须低温保存！）</span>

# 断肢伤口处理 🩹

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-8">

<div v-click>

### 🩸 伤口止血

**止血方法：**

1. **直接压迫止血** - 用无菌纱布压住伤口，持续按压 10-15 分钟
2. **抬高伤肢** - 伤口高于心脏，减少血流量
3. **必要时使用止血带** - 仅用于<span v-mark.underline.red>大出血</span>且其他方法无效，记录时间！每 40-60 分钟松开一次

<div class="mt-4 p-3 bg-red-50 rounded text-center">
⚠️ 止血是第一要务！
</div>

</div>

<div v-click>

### 🛡️ 伤口保护

**保护措施：**

- ✅ **覆盖无菌纱布** - 保护伤口，防止感染
- ✅ **固定伤肢** - 防止二次损伤
- ✅ **保暖** - 防止休克，但不要过热
- ❌ **不要自行包扎过紧** - 会影响血液循环

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6" v-click>

### 🚑 转运注意事项

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-4">

<div class="text-center">

**👤 伤者优先**

稳定生命体征

</div>

<div class="text-center">

**❄️ 断肢保冷**

持续低温保存

</div>

<div class="text-center">

**⏱️ 争分夺秒**

尽快送达医院

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-3 bg-yellow-50 rounded text-center">
📞 <strong>提前通知医院</strong>准备再植手术
</div>

</div>
