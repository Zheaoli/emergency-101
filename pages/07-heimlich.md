# 特殊人群的海姆立克 👶🤰

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-6 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-pink-50 rounded-lg">

<h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-pink-700 text-lg text-center">👶 婴儿（<1岁）</h3>

<div class="text-center mb-4">
<div class="text-6xl">👶</div>
</div>

<div class="space-y-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-2 bg-white rounded">
<strong class="text-pink-600">1. 拍背法</strong>
<ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
<li>• 让婴儿<strong>趴</strong>在前臂上</li>
<li>• 头部<strong>低于</strong>身体</li>
<li>• 用手掌根拍打<strong>肩胛骨之间</strong></li>
<li>• 拍 <span v-mark.circle.pink>5 次</span></li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="p-2 bg-white rounded">
<strong class="text-pink-600">2. 胸部按压法</strong>
<ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
<li>• 翻转婴儿，<strong>仰卧</strong>在前臂上</li>
<li>• 头部仍然<strong>低于</strong>身体</li>
<li>• 两指按压<strong>胸骨中下段</strong></li>
<li>• 按压 <span v-mark.circle.pink>5 次</span></li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="p-2 bg-red-100 rounded text-center">
<strong class="text-red-700 text-xs">⚠️ 交替进行</strong>
<p class="text-xs mt-1">拍背 5 次 → 胸压 5 次 → 重复</p>
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-2 bg-yellow-100 rounded text-xs text-center">
🚫 <strong>不要</strong>对婴儿使用成人方法！
</div>

</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-purple-50 rounded-lg">

<div class="text-6xl">🧒</div>
</div>

<div class="space-y-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-2 bg-white rounded">

<p class="text-xs mt-1 text-gray-600">但要注意：</p>
</div>

<div class="p-2 bg-blue-100 rounded">

<ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
<li>• 根据孩子<strong>体型调整</strong>力度</li>

<strong class="text-xs">🎯 拳头位置</strong>

<li>• 双臂从<strong>腋下</strong>环抱</li>
<li>• 一手握拳放在<strong>胸骨中央</strong></li>
