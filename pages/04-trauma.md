# 外伤处理步骤 📋

<v-clicks>

### 1️⃣ 止血

<div class="p-4 bg-red-50 rounded-lg mb-4">
  <strong>直接压迫法（首选）</strong>
  <ul class="text-sm mt-2 space-y-1">
    <li>• 用干净纱布/毛巾压住伤口</li>
    <li>• 持续按压 <span v-mark.circle.red>5-15 分钟</span></li>
    <li>• 不要频繁查看（会影响凝血）</li>
  </ul>
</div>

### 2️⃣ 清洁

<div class="p-4 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg mb-4">
  <strong>正确清洁方法</strong>
  <ul class="text-sm mt-2 space-y-1">
    <li>✅ 生理盐水/清水冲洗</li>
    <li>✅ 从伤口中心向外清洁</li>
    <li>❌ 不要用酒精直接冲洗（疼且伤组织）</li>
  </ul>
</div>

### 3️⃣ 包扎

<div class="p-4 bg-green-50 rounded-lg">
  <strong>包扎注意事项</strong>
  <ul class="text-sm mt-2 space-y-1">
    <li>• 使用无菌纱布</li>
    <li>• 松紧适度（不影响血液循环）</li>
    <li>• 固定牢靠</li>
  </ul>
</div>

</v-clicks>

::right::

<div v-click="1">

<div class="mt-8 p-4 bg-yellow-100 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
  <h3 class="font-bold text-yellow-800 mb-3">⚠️ 常见错误做法</h3>

  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">

  <div class="flex items-start gap-2" v-click>
    <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <strong>用嘴吸毒</strong>
      <p class="text-xs mt-1">（你以为你是黄蓉？口腔细菌会感染伤口！）</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="flex items-start gap-2" v-click>
    <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <strong>伤口撒盐/糖/草木灰</strong>
      <p class="text-xs mt-1">（这不是在做腌菜！会增加感染风险）</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="flex items-start gap-2" v-click>
    <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <strong>用嘴吹伤口</strong>
      <p class="text-xs mt-1">（细菌：谢谢投喂）</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="flex items-start gap-2" v-click>
    <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <strong>自己拔出深插异物</strong>

  </div>

  <div class="flex items-start gap-2" v-click>
    <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <strong>伤口用酒精/碘酒直接灌</strong>
