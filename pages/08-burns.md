# 烧烫伤分级 📊

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-6 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-yellow-50 rounded-lg border-2 border-yellow-300">

<h3 class="text-center mb-3 text-yellow-700 font-bold text-lg">Ⅰ度烧伤（轻度）</h3>

<div class="text-center text-5xl mb-3">😊</div>

<div class="text-sm space-y-2">
<div><strong>特征：</strong></div>
<ul class="text-xs space-y-1 ml-4">
<li>• 皮肤<span v-mark.highlight.red>发红</span></li>
<li>• 有<span v-mark.highlight.yellow>疼痛</span>感</li>
<li>• <strong>无水泡</strong></li>
<li>• 触摸温热</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-white rounded">
<strong class="text-xs">常见场景：</strong>
<p class="text-xs mt-1">轻微晒伤、短时间接触热物</p>
</div>

<div class="mt-2 p-2 bg-green-100 rounded text-center">
<strong class="text-green-700 text-xs">✅ 可自行处理</strong>
<p class="text-xs mt-1">一般 3-7 天痊愈</p>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-orange-50 rounded-lg border-2 border-orange-300">

<h3 class="text-center mb-3 text-orange-700 font-bold text-lg">Ⅱ度烧伤（中度）</h3>

<div class="text-center text-5xl mb-3">😰</div>

<div class="text-sm space-y-2">
<div><strong>特征：</strong></div>
<ul class="text-xs space-y-1 ml-4">
<li>• 皮肤<span v-mark.highlight.red>红肿</span></li>
<li>• <strong>有水泡</strong> 💧</li>
<li>• <span v-mark.highlight.yellow>剧烈疼痛</span></li>
<li>• 渗液</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-white rounded">
<strong class="text-xs">分为：</strong>
<ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1">
<li>• <strong>浅Ⅱ度</strong>：水泡大，痛感强</li>
<li>• <strong>深Ⅱ度</strong>：水泡小，基底发白</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="mt-2 p-2 bg-yellow-100 rounded text-center">
<strong class="text-yellow-700 text-xs">⚠️ 建议就医</strong>
<p class="text-xs mt-1">2-4 周痊愈，可能留疤</p>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-2 border-red-300">

<h3 class="text-center mb-3 text-red-700 font-bold text-lg">Ⅲ度烧伤（重度）</h3>

<div class="text-center text-5xl mb-3">😱</div>

<div class="text-sm space-y-2">
<div><strong>特征：</strong></div>
<ul class="text-xs space-y-1 ml-4">
<li>• 皮肤<span v-mark.highlight.red>焦黑/发白</span></li>
<li>• <strong>无水泡</strong></li>
<li>• <span v-mark.highlight.red>反而不太痛</span>（神经坏死）</li>
<li>• 皮肤僵硬</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-white rounded">
<strong class="text-xs">危险信号：</strong>
<p class="text-xs mt-1">全层皮肤坏死，可能危及生命</p>
</div>

<div class="mt-2 p-2 bg-red-200 rounded text-center">
<strong class="text-red-700 text-xs">🚨 立即 120！</strong>
<p class="text-xs mt-1">需要专业救治</p>
</div>

</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg text-center" v-click>

<strong class="text-blue-700">💡 判断技巧：</strong>

<span v-mark.underline.blue>有水泡 = Ⅱ度，发黑/发白 = Ⅲ度，只是红 = Ⅰ度</span>

</div>

# 五字口诀详解 🎯

<v-clicks>

<div class="p-4 bg-red-50 rounded-lg mb-3">
  <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">💧</span>
    <span>冲 - 冷水冲洗</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>✅ <strong>流动冷水</strong>冲洗 <span v-mark.circle.red>15-30 分钟</span></div>
    <div>✅ 水温 <strong>15-20°C</strong>（不是冰水！）</div>
    <div>✅ 降低伤口温度，减少损伤</div>
    <div>⚠️ 面积大时注意<strong>保暖</strong>（防休克）</div>
  </div>
  <div class="mt-2 p-2 bg-white rounded text-xs text-center">
    💡 这是<span v-mark.highlight.red>最重要</span>的一步！
  </div>
</div>

<div class="p-4 bg-orange-50 rounded-lg mb-3">
  <h3 class="font-bold text-orange-700 text-lg mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">👕</span>
    <span>脱 - 脱掉衣物</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>✅ <strong>在水中</strong>小心脱掉衣物</div>
    <div>✅ 如果粘连，用<strong>剪刀剪开</strong></div>
    <div>❌ <strong>不要硬扯！</strong>会撕掉皮肤</div>
    <div>⚠️ 戒指、手表等也要取下（会肿胀）</div>
  </div>
  <div class="mt-2 p-2 bg-white rounded text-xs text-center">
    ⚠️ 粘连严重的直接去医院处理
  </div>
</div>

<div class="p-4 bg-yellow-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold text-yellow-700 text-lg mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">🧊</span>
    <span>泡 - 冷水浸泡</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>✅ 继续用<strong>冷水浸泡</strong> 10-30 分钟</div>
    <div>✅ 进一步<strong>降温止痛</strong></div>
    <div>⚠️ 如伤口较大，<strong>跳过此步</strong></div>
    <div>⚠️ 注意保暖，别让患者发冷</div>
  </div>
</div>

</v-clicks>

::right::

<v-clicks>

<div class="p-4 bg-green-50 rounded-lg mb-3">
  <h3 class="font-bold text-green-700 text-lg mb-2 flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">🩹</span>
    <span>盖 - 覆盖保护</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>✅ 用<strong>无菌纱布</strong>或干净布料覆盖</div>
    <div>✅ 保持<strong>松散</strong>，不要包太紧</div>
    <div>✅ 防止<strong>污染和感染</strong></div>
    <div>❌ <strong>不要</strong>涂任何东西（牙膏/酱油等）</div>
  </div>
  <div class="mt-2 p-2 bg-white rounded text-xs text-center">
    🚫 民间偏方害人不浅！
  </div>
</div>

# 烧烫伤的错误处理方式 ❌

<div class="text-center mb-6 text-lg text-red-600 font-bold" v-click>
<span v-mark.highlight.yellow>这些"民间偏方"千万别用！</span>
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-500">
  <div class="flex items-start gap-3 mb-4">
    <span class="text-4xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg">涂牙膏</h3>
      <p class="text-sm mt-2">为什么不行？</p>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 不能<span v-mark.highlight.red>降温</span>，反而<strong>保温</strong>！</li>
        <li>• 会<strong>污染伤口</strong>，增加感染风险</li>
        <li>• 清洗时会<strong>二次伤害</strong></li>
        <li>• 妨碍医生判断伤情</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded text-center text-xs">
    🦷 牙膏是用来刷牙的，不是用来涂伤口的！
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-500">
  <div class="flex items-start gap-3 mb-4">
    <span class="text-4xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg">涂酱油/醋</h3>
      <p class="text-sm mt-2">为什么不行？</p>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 含有<strong>大量细菌</strong></li>
        <li>• 会<span v-mark.highlight.red>加深伤口颜色</span>，影响判断</li>
        <li>• 可能导致<strong>严重感染</strong></li>
        <li>• 无任何治疗作用</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded text-center text-xs">
    🍶 你这是在做红烧肉还是在救人？
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-500">
  <div class="flex items-start gap-3 mb-4">
    <span class="text-4xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg">涂香油/菜油</h3>
      <p class="text-sm mt-2">为什么不行？</p>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 油脂会<span v-mark.highlight.red>锁住热量</span></li>
        <li>• 加重<strong>热损伤</strong></li>
        <li>• 形成油性保护膜，<strong>不易清洗</strong></li>
        <li>• 阻碍伤口散热和愈合</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded text-center text-xs">
    🍳 伤口不是炒菜锅！
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-500">
  <div class="flex items-start gap-3 mb-4">
    <span class="text-4xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg">涂盐/糖</h3>
      <p class="text-sm mt-2">为什么不行？</p>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• <span v-mark.highlight.red>剧烈刺激</span>，疼痛加倍</li>
        <li>• 会<strong>脱水</strong>，加重损伤</li>
        <li>• 可能导致<strong>组织坏死</strong></li>
        <li>• 完全没有治疗作用</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded text-center text-xs">
    🧂 这不是在腌咸菜啊喂！
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-500">
  <div class="flex items-start gap-3 mb-4">
    <span class="text-4xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg">冰块直接敷</h3>
      <p class="text-sm mt-2">为什么不行？</p>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 会造成<span v-mark.highlight.red>冻伤</span></li>
        <li>• 血管<strong>急剧收缩</strong>，影响愈合</li>
        <li>• 可能导致<strong>组织坏死</strong></li>
        <li>• 用<strong>冷水</strong>就够了</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded text-center text-xs">
    ❄️ 从烧伤变冻伤，这波操作很秀！
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-red-500">
  <div class="flex items-start gap-3 mb-4">
    <span class="text-4xl">❌</span>
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-bold text-red-700 text-lg">挑破水泡</h3>
      <p class="text-sm mt-2">为什么不行？</p>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 水泡是<strong>天然保护层</strong></li>
        <li>• 挑破会<span v-mark.highlight.red>增加感染</span>风险</li>
        <li>• 疼痛加剧</li>
        <li>• 让医生处理更安全</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded text-center text-xs">
    💧 别手痒！水泡有它的作用！
  </div>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-5 bg-yellow-50 rounded-lg text-center" v-click>
  <p class="text-xl font-bold text-yellow-700 mb-2">
    🚫 <span v-mark.circle.red>记住：烧烫伤后只用冷水，其他什么都别涂！</span>
  </p>
  <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">民间偏方不是经验，是教训！</p>
</div>

# 特殊烧烫伤处理 ⚡🧪

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-yellow-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-yellow-700 text-lg flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">⚡</span>
    <span>电烧伤</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">
    <div class="p-2 bg-red-100 rounded">
      <strong class="text-red-700">⚠️ 第一步：断电源！</strong>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 关闭电源开关</li>
        <li>• 用<strong>绝缘物</strong>（木棍/塑料）挑开电线</li>
        <li>• <strong>不要</strong>直接接触触电者</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded">
      <strong>处理要点：</strong>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 电烧伤<strong>外轻内重</strong></li>
        <li>• 可能有<span v-mark.highlight.yellow>内脏损伤</span></li>
        <li>• 检查<strong>呼吸心跳</strong></li>
        <li>• <strong>必要时 CPR</strong></li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="p-2 bg-blue-100 rounded text-center">
      <strong class="text-blue-700">🚨 必须立即 120</strong>
      <p class="text-xs mt-1">即使表面烧伤轻微</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-purple-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-purple-700 text-lg flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">🧪</span>
    <span>化学烧伤</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">
    <div class="p-2 bg-red-100 rounded">
      <strong class="text-red-700">⚠️ 立即处理</strong>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• <strong>大量清水</strong>冲洗 <span v-mark.circle.purple>20-30 分钟</span></li>
        <li>• 边冲边<strong>脱掉</strong>污染衣物</li>
        <li>• <strong>不要</strong>试图中和（会放热）</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded">
      <strong>特殊情况：</strong>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• <strong>生石灰</strong>：先<span v-mark.highlight.purple>刷掉粉末</span>，再冲水</li>
        <li>• <strong>浓硫酸</strong>：先<span v-mark.highlight.purple>擦拭</span>（少量），再大量冲水</li>
        <li>• <strong>眼部</strong>：侧头冲洗，<strong>从内向外</strong></li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="p-2 bg-blue-100 rounded text-center">
      <strong class="text-blue-700">🚨 立即 120</strong>
      <p class="text-xs mt-1">带上化学品标签/瓶子</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-green-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-green-700 text-lg flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">☀️</span>
    <span>日晒伤</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">
    <div class="p-2 bg-white rounded">
      <strong>轻度晒伤：</strong>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 冷水敷或冷水澡</li>
        <li>• 涂抹<strong>芦荟胶</strong></li>
        <li>• 多喝水补充水分</li>
        <li>• 避免再次暴晒</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="p-2 bg-yellow-100 rounded">
      <strong>严重晒伤（大水泡）：</strong>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-1 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• 按烧伤处理</li>
        <li>• <strong>不要</strong>挑破水泡</li>
        <li>• 尽快就医</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-orange-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-orange-700 text-lg flex items-center gap-2">
    <span class="text-3xl">🔥</span>
    <span>着火时</span>
  </h3>
  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">
    <div class="p-2 bg-red-100 rounded">
      <strong class="text-red-700">⚠️ 衣服着火</strong>
      <p class="text-xs mt-1">记住口诀：</p>
      <div class="mt-2 text-center">
        <div class="text-lg font-bold">
          <span v-mark.highlight.red>停</span> -
          <span v-mark.highlight.orange>躺</span> -
          <span v-mark.highlight.yellow>滚</span>
        </div>
      </div>
      <ul class="text-xs mt-2 space-y-1 ml-4">
        <li>• <strong>停</strong>：停止奔跑</li>
        <li>• <strong>躺</strong>：立即躺下</li>
        <li>• <strong>滚</strong>：来回翻滚灭火</li>
      </ul>
