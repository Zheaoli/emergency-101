# 止血四大法 🎯

<v-clicks>

### 1️⃣ 直接压迫止血法（首选！）

<div class="p-4 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg mb-4">
  <div class="font-bold mb-2 text-blue-700">🏆 最有效、最常用</div>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>✅ 用<strong>干净纱布/毛巾</strong>直接压住伤口</div>
    <div>✅ 持续按压 <span v-mark.underline.red>10-15 分钟</span></div>
    <div>✅ <strong>不要频繁查看</strong>（影响凝血）</div>
    <div>✅ 如果血浸透，再加一层纱布（不要移除旧纱布）</div>
  </div>
  <div class="mt-3 p-2 bg-white rounded text-xs text-center">
    💡 90% 的出血都能用这招搞定
  </div>
</div>

### 2️⃣ 抬高肢体法

<div class="p-4 bg-green-50 rounded-lg mb-4">
  <div class="font-bold mb-2 text-green-700">📈 辅助方法</div>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>✅ 伤口部位<strong>抬高</strong>超过心脏</div>
    <div>✅ 减少血液流向伤口</div>
    <div>✅ <strong>配合</strong>直接压迫使用</div>
    <div>⚠️ 怀疑骨折时<strong>不使用</strong>此法</div>
  </div>
</div>

</v-clicks>

::right::

<v-clicks>

### 3️⃣ 压迫止血点法

<div class="p-4 bg-purple-50 rounded-lg mb-4">
  <div class="font-bold mb-2 text-purple-700">🎯 需要一定技巧</div>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>• 压迫伤口<strong>近心端</strong>的动脉</div>
    <div>• 常用止血点：</div>
    <div class="ml-4 space-y-1">
      <div>- 颞动脉（头部出血）</div>
      <div>- 颈动脉（面部出血）⚠️</div>
      <div>- 肱动脉（上臂出血）</div>
      <div>- 股动脉（大腿出血）</div>
    </div>
    <div class="text-xs text-red-600 mt-2">
      ⚠️ 颈动脉只能压一侧！双侧压迫会致命！
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

### 4️⃣ 止血带法（最后手段！）

<div class="p-4 bg-red-50 rounded-lg border-2 border-red-300">
  <div class="font-bold mb-2 text-red-700">🚨 紧急情况专用</div>
  <div class="text-sm space-y-2">
    <div>⚠️ <strong>仅用于</strong>四肢大出血且其他方法无效时</div>
    <div>✅ 绑在伤口<strong>近心端</strong> 5-10cm 处</div>
    <div>✅ 记录<strong>上止血带时间</strong>（重要！）</div>
    <div>⚠️ 每 <span v-mark.circle.red>40-60 分钟</span>松开 2-3 分钟</div>
    <div class="text-xs text-red-600 mt-2">
      🚫 止血带绑太久会导致肢体坏死！
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

</v-clicks>

# 止血带使用图解 🎨

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-green-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-green-700 flex items-center gap-2">
    <span>✅</span> 正确使用方法
  </h3>
  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="font-bold">1.</span>
      <span>在伤口<strong>近心端</strong> 5-10cm 处</span>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="font-bold">2.</span>
      <span>垫上<strong>软布</strong>（保护皮肤）</span>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="font-bold">3.</span>
      <span>绑紧至<strong>出血停止</strong></span>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="font-bold">4.</span>
      <span>标记<strong>时间</strong>（可以写在伤者额头上）</span>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="font-bold">5.</span>
      <span>每 40-60 分钟<strong>松开</strong> 2-3 分钟</span>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="mt-4 p-3 bg-white rounded text-center">
    <div class="text-6xl mb-2">🦵</div>
    <div class="text-xs">止血带位置：伤口近心端</div>
    <div class="text-xs text-gray-500">（也就是离心脏更近的一侧）</div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-red-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-red-700 flex items-center gap-2">
    <span>❌</span> 常见错误
  </h3>
  <div class="space-y-3 text-sm">
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
      <div>
        <strong>绑在伤口远心端</strong>
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">（没用！血还是会流到伤口）</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
      <div>
        <strong>绑得太松</strong>
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">（只阻断静脉，反而出血更多）</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
      <div>
        <strong>用细绳/铁丝</strong>
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">（会切入皮肤造成二次伤害）</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
      <div>
        <strong>长时间不松开</strong>
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">（超过 2 小时可能导致肢体坏死）</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-2xl">❌</span>
      <div>
        <strong>用在颈部</strong>
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">（会窒息！止血带只能用于四肢！）</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-yellow-100 rounded-lg text-center" v-click>
  <p class="font-bold text-lg">
    ⚠️ <span v-mark.underline.red>记住：止血带是"双刃剑"！</span>
  </p>
  <p class="text-sm mt-2">能用直接压迫就别用止血带</p>
</div>

# 特殊部位止血 🎯

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-blue-700">👃 鼻出血</h3>
  <div class="space-y-2 text-sm">
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-green-600 text-xl">✅</span>
      <div>
        <strong>低头</strong>（让血流出来）
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">不要仰头！会导致血液倒流</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-green-600 text-xl">✅</span>
      <div>
        <strong>捏住鼻翼</strong> 10-15 分钟
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">用拇指和食指捏紧两侧鼻翼</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-green-600 text-xl">✅</span>
      <div>
        <strong>冷敷额头</strong>
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">帮助血管收缩</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="mt-3 p-2 bg-white rounded text-xs text-center">
    🤔 记忆口诀：<span v-mark.highlight.blue>低头、捏鼻、冷敷</span>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-purple-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-purple-700">🦷 拔牙后出血</h3>
  <div class="space-y-2 text-sm">
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-green-600 text-xl">✅</span>
      <div>
        <strong>咬住纱布</strong> 30-60 分钟
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">用力咬紧，不要说话</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-green-600 text-xl">✅</span>
      <div>
        <strong>24 小时内</strong>不要漱口
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">避免破坏血凝块</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex items-start gap-2">
      <span class="text-red-600 text-xl">❌</span>
      <div>
        <strong>不要</strong>频繁吐口水
        <p class="text-xs mt-1">会增加出血</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>

<div v-click>
<div class="p-5 bg-green-50 rounded-lg">
  <h3 class="font-bold mb-3 text-green-700">🫀 内出血（严重！）</h3>
  <div class="space-y-2 text-sm">
    <div class="p-2 bg-yellow-100 rounded">
      <strong>疑似内出血症状：</strong>

        <li>• 脉搏快而弱</li>
        <li>• 意识模糊</li>
        <li>• 呕血或便血</li>

        <li>• 让伤者<strong>平躺</strong></li>
        <li>• 保持安静和温暖</li>
        <li>• <strong>禁食禁水</strong></li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
</div>
